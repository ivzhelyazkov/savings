Welcome to Sloth Savings!

Sloth Savings was designed by me as a SoftUni student while learning Django.

You can create your profile and set your initial budget. You can edit your profile in Profile Settings. There's an option to change your password or delete your profile too.

When you are logged in you can add, edit and delete your transactions. Transactions are two kinds - Incoming payments and Expenses. The app tracks your transactions and offers you all-time and monthly reports.
