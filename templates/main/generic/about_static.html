Welcome to Sloth Savings!

Sloth Savings was created by IvZh as a Django project for a SoftUni exam while learning about Python Frameworks. It tracks your all-time and monthly budget and expenses and provides you with reports by type and category.

You can register for a new profile or log in to your existing account. Click on Profile Settings to edit your profile. There you can also change your password or even delete your profile.

Click on Add Expense or Add Incoming to enter your transaction into the database. The app automatically calculates your savings and can provide you with lists of income/expenditures. Click on category or type to see all transactions for the period.

Monthly report provides you with a list of your transactions for the current month so you can better track your budget. You can click on each individual transaction details and edit or delete it.
